🎤 AI Communication Analysis

Our AI-powered communication analysis evaluates how effectively you present your ideas during an interview. It analyzes multiple aspects of your speech such as speaking speed, clarity, confidence, and delivery quality. This helps identify strengths in your communication style and highlights areas where improvement can increase your chances of success in technical interviews.

📊

Words Per Minute

Measures your speaking speed to ensure your explanation is clear and neither too fast nor too slow.

🗣

Fluency Score

Evaluates how smoothly you speak without unnecessary pauses or interruptions.

🎯

Tone & Intonation

Analyzes voice modulation to determine whether your speech sounds engaging and natural.

💪

Confidence Level

Detects confidence in your voice delivery while explaining technical concepts.

⚠

Filler Words

Identifies filler words such as "um", "uh", and "like" that may reduce clarity.

⭐

Communication Score

Combines all metrics to generate an overall evaluation of your interview communication performance.
